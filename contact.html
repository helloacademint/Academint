FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I receive the session recordings?

Recordings are shared via email within 24 hours of each session. You'll have lifetime access to all recordings and course materials.

Is the workshop suitable for beginners?

The workshop is designed for beginner to intermediate level. Basic familiarity with statistics is helpful but not required. R will be taught from scratch.

Will I receive a certificate?

Yes! All participants who attend the sessions receive an official certificate of completion from Academint.

What if I miss a live session?

No problem — all sessions are recorded and shared promptly. You'll never miss out on content regardless of your schedule.

How do I pay for the workshop?

Indian participants can pay via Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking). International participants can pay via Stripe. Links will be shared shortly.

Can organisations enrol multiple participants?

Absolutely. For group enrolments or institutional partnerships, please email us directly for special pricing and arrangements.
